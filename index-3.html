THE BIG ONE
Push your luck on the high banks
  • You are running up front on a superspeedway. The big number is the points you would lock in if you pit right now.
  • Every round you stay out, that number can climb. Better track position and stage points are worth real points.
  • But the Big One is coming. Somewhere in the danger window, a multi car wreck collects everyone still hanging it out, and it craters your finish.
  • Read the danger meter. The mark on the bar is the smart bail line. Pit before the window and you keep every point.
  • Here is the edge: if you survive the window, the meter falls. The pack thins, the track opens, and a brave driver can push to the front for a much bigger finish. The last lap spikes again, so going for the win is its own gamble.
  • Set your posture to gain spots or play it safe. Beat the target to keep your streak. The big scores belong to whoever reads the window best.
